Boboiboy The Movie

A group of alien treasure hunters named The Tengkotak has arrived on earth and kidnapped Ochobot (Mohd Fathi b Diaz) in order to use him to locate an ancient and powerful Sfera Kuasa hiding on earth. BoBoiBoy (Nur Fathiah bt Diaz) and his super friends must now race against time to save Ochobot and uncover the secrets behind the Sfera Kuasa. Their journey will take them on an adventure filled with action, comedy, and beautiful locales.
